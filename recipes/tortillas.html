Corn Tortillas

Most dishes in Mexican cuisine rely on the humble tortilla as the preferred method of delivery! From tacos, to mole, any dish will benefit from adding this recipe on the side!

Ingredients

  • 1¾ cups masa harina
  • 1⅛ cups hot water

Steps

  1. Mix together the masa harina and hot water in a medium bowl until thoroughly combined.
  2. Transfer the dough to a clean, flat work surface and knead until smooth and pliable.
  3. Cover the dough tightly with plastic wrap and allow to sit at room temp for 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat a cast iron skillet or griddle over medium-high heat.
  5. Divide the dough into 15 equal-sized balls.
  6. Using a tortilla press, rolling pin, or your hands, press each ball flat between two sheets of plastic wrap.
  7. Cook on the preheated skillet until browned and slightly puffy. (Around 30 seconds each side)
